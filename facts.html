Did You Know?

Mind-blowing facts about the world's most mysterious civilization.

No kingship

Unlike Egypt or Mesopotamia, archaeologists found no giant king palaces, suggesting society may have been more organized and equal.

Smart Bathrooms

Almost every house in Mohenjo-Daro had its own private bathroom and toilet connected to a city-wide drainage system.

The Unsolved Mystery

The writing of the civilization still cannot be fully read today, making it one of history's biggest mysteries.

Balance

Most cities used bricks of the same size, showing advanced planning and measurement systems.

Ancient Gamers

Archaeologists found dice and game boards, showing people enjoyed games thousands of years ago.

Ancient Dentists

At Mehrgarh, scientists found evidence of people drilling teeth around 7,000 years ago — one of the earliest forms of dentistry.

Tidal Dockyards

Lothal possibly had one of the world's earliest dockyards for ships and trade.

A Peaceful People

Very few weapons or battle scenes were found, so historians think the civilization may have been relatively peaceful compared to others of that time.

People loved jewellery

Men and women wore necklaces, bangles, beads, and ornaments made from gold, shells, and stones.

The Disappearance

The civilization didn't die in a war; many believe they vanished because the rivers changed course or dried up.
